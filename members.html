이보아 (Boa Rhee)

중앙대학교 예술공학부 교수 / Lab Director
Boa Rhee is a professor and researcher specializing in museum studies, digital media, and audience experience, with a particular focus on the intersection of art, technology, and data-driven cultural analysis. Her research spans museum visitor experience, immersive and hybrid exhibitions, artificial intelligence in museums, and visual culture in digital platforms, including social media. She has published extensively on museum innovation, AI-based exhibition analysis, and audience research methodologies, and has served as a peer reviewer for international journals in the fields of digital culture and media studies. Her work bridges theoretical inquiry and practice, contributing to contemporary discussions on Museum 2.0 and 3.0, participatory culture, and the evolving role of cultural institutions in the digital age.
박물관·미술관 경영 및 몰입형 미디어 전시 기획 전문가로서, 예술과 기술, 그리고 데이터 기반 문화 분석의 접점을 탐구합니다. 인공지능 기반 전시 분석과 관람객 행동 모델링을 선도하며, 뮤지엄 2.0과 3.0, 참여형 문화 등 디지털 시대 문화 기관의 진화하는 역할에 대해 학문적 담론과 실무적 실천을 병행하고 있습니다.
